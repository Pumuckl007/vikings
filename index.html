      We are a student-run team from La Jolla High School. Every year we build a new robot for the FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC). The challenge is always different and involves collaborating with the other team’s robots. FIRST is a non-profit organization that encourage students to pursue engineering careers.


      As a robotics team we need money to do almost anything. We have to cover the bills from registration to the metal for the robot. A detailed view of the needs is available on our needs page. We completely rely on your donations to continue to function. You can support us by visiting our donations page or by donating online at foundationofljhs.com.
